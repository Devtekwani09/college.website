IDEA LAB Department

About

The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has launched the IDEA (Innovation, Development, Entrepreneurship and Advancement) Lab to promote innovation and entrepreneurship among students in technical education institutions. The IDEA Lab is designed to provide a platform for students to conceptualize and develop their ideas into sustainable projects and startups.

Objective

The main objective of the IDEA Lab is to foster a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship among students in technical education institutions. The lab aims to create an environment that is conducive to the development of new ideas and the creation of new ventures. Additionally, the IDEA Lab will provide students with the resources and support they need to turn their ideas into successful projects and startups.

Execution

The IDEA Lab will be set up in technical education institutions across the country. It will be equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and resources to support the development of student ideas and projects. The lab will also provide students with access to mentors, experts, and industry leaders who can provide guidance and support throughout the development process. The IDEA lab will be a place for Students and Researchers to engage in experimentation, collaboration and co-creation, it will enable them to design, develop, test and validate new products, services, and business models that lead to innovation and entrepreneurship. The IDEA Lab will organize various programs and events throughout the year, including workshops, hackathons, and incubation programs, to encourage students to participate in innovation and entrepreneurship activities. Additionally, the IDEA Lab will offer funding and resources for students to turn their ideas into prototypes and test them in the market.

HOD Message

Er. Udit Mamodiya

HOD,
AICTE IDEA LAB

I am delighted to welcome you to the IDEA Lab website, an initiative by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) to foster innovation, development, entrepreneurship, and advancement among our students. This platform aims to ignite the spirit of creativity and provide the necessary resources for transforming ideas into reality.

At IDEA Lab, we believe that innovation is the key driver of progress and prosperity. We are committed to creating an ecosystem where students can unleash their potential and channel their passion towards solving real-world problems. Through this lab, we aim to nurture the entrepreneurial mindset and equip our students with the skills needed to excel in the dynamic and competitive global landscape.

The IDEA Lab is a collaborative space where students and researchers can come together to experiment, collaborate, and co-create. It offers state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge technology, and a supportive environment that encourages exploration and learning. Our dedicated team of mentors, experts, and industry leaders is here to guide and inspire you on your entrepreneurial journey.

Our objective is to provide you with the tools and knowledge required to transform your innovative ideas into viable projects and successful startups. We offer various programs, workshops, hackathons, and incubation opportunities to help you develop your entrepreneurial acumen, enhance your technical skills, and gain hands-on experience. Moreover, we provide funding and resources to turn your ideas into prototypes and test them in the market.

I invite all students, faculty, and stakeholders to actively participate in the IDEA Lab and contribute to the vibrant innovation ecosystem we are building. Together, let us create a culture of innovation, where curiosity thrives, ideas flourish, and breakthroughs happen.

I am confident that with your enthusiasm, creativity, and dedication, we will witness remarkable achievements and entrepreneurial success stories from our institution. The IDEA Lab is here to empower you and be the catalyst for your entrepreneurial dreams.

Let us embark on this exciting journey of innovation and entrepreneurship. Welcome to IDEA Lab! .
