About

The Department of Computer Engineering was incepted in the year 2007 with the vision, “To create an environment in which new ideas, research and technology development and the technocrats and innovators of tomorrow become competent to face the global challenges”, In these academic years Department of Computer Engineering (CS), PIET has achieved lot of success and earn many achievements.

I am glad to inform that Department has been accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in year 2017-18 and got copmpliance till session 2023-24it and so now in elite list of departments in India which has got this accreditation. Because of this it is now recognized to confirm the best practices and benchmarks in engineering. It was possible because of dedicated mentors and bright students. Department has got always good number of faculty members and technical assistants all Faculty members are M. Tech. 40 % teachers have always been PHD holders. Because Department believes in outcome-based education and result oriented approach it could attract intelligent students available in the state and outside. Currently 867 students are enrolled in the Department. The admission in the Department from 2007 to 2022 has been consistent and has always been morethan 95%. Department has best of infrastructure in terms of thirteen computer labs, ten classrooms, 3 Electronic lab and 19 faculty rooms . Department has got one state of art online lab for conducting online test and for research and development. The honor of being largest and most responsible Department of college it has always got highest result and result has always been consistent and encouraging. Due to its working methodologies and industry oriented approach it has converted hard work of students into strong placements.

The Department has got highest placement in PIET. In terms of percentage, placement are increasing and many students got placed in big IT Giants like Infosys, TCS, Capegemini, IBM,Evolphin, Naggaro, Appirio every year. To be recognized by industrial houses as source of consultancy and process management department always nurture students to develop innovative projects using cutting edge technologies. Students develop project in each year of study starting from I year in the field of machine learning, Cloud Computing, Data Science, Big data, sales Force, IoT, image processing and Industry oriented projects using PHP, Python, JAVA. To showcase projects Department of Computer Engineering organizes project exhibition “PRAKALP-” every for the students of all years. Computer Engineering, PIET in which around 60 projects developed under NSP. The Department has started a new initiative in 2nd and 3rd year by introducing the concept of Non-Syllabus Project. In this scheme, all students of II and III year develop projects of real-world using Python and PHP. Final Year students are also developing Projects. Department holds its position of being the most innovative and research-oriented workplace so around 40% students of final year publish review/ Research papers in area of smart computation and technology and Every faculty member publish 2 papers each year.

To encourage and to provide platform l International Conferences on Smart Computation and Technology (ICSCT) have been organized. To further strengthen the research and Development, the Department is considering technical seminars as research paper presentation under strict mentorship of faculties, alumni’s and industrial experts. Teachers and students have conducted many workshops to provide insight on new trends & technology and to bridge the gap between industry and academia. The workshops are like Artificial intelligence, Python Programming, Android Programming, PHP MySQL and many more of similar kind. It has been the need of the hour to get exposure of new technology to survive in evolutionary industry so Department has introduced IBM Business Intelligence, IBM Cloud Computing and IBM IoT courses and Machine Learning Courses for all the students. To strengthen the skill set of teachers and students, the Department organizes student development and Faculty development programs with the help of alumns and industrial experts.

To prepare students for placement department conducts many Placement drives, coding competitions; aptitude tests, sessions by FACE and technical sessions. Students also take part in national Competitions such as TCS Codevita, Aspiration 2020 and similar coding and website Designing Competion. Department also encourages to participate and contribute to outside world and society. In order to improve transparency and agility in the scheme of marks allocation Department has launched marks allotment system which is web portal to allocate marks of various activities and processes under common pool and DECA. It has taken initiative to start own portal for the home assignment submission and awarding marks.
